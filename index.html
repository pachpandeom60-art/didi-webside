Step 1

The Harvest

We begin by hand-selecting the finest, sun-ripened turmeric roots, fresh red manjistha, and the most fragrant rose petals. Only the purest botanicals make the cut, honoring centuries of Ayurvedic tradition.

Step 2

Stone Grinding

Using the ancient silbatta (stone grinder), our artisans crush the raw herbs into a potent, nutrient-rich paste. This slow, cold-press method ensures no beneficial properties are lost to heat.

Step 3

The Final Creation

The potent herbal paste is gently folded into our luxurious goat milk base and poured into molds. After curing, it becomes the Ayuroma bar—a pure, golden block of skincare perfection.
